Have questions about Terminowo?

How does document scanning work?
Open the app, point your camera at any paper document, and tap Scan. Terminowo uses OCR to read the expiry date and pre-fills the details for you automatically.
What types of documents can I add?
Insurance policies, driver's licenses, vehicle inspections, rental agreements, subscriptions, payment receipts — anything with an expiry date.
Can I add documents manually without scanning?
Yes! You can enter document details manually if you prefer not to use the camera.
How do reminders work?
Choose when you want to be reminded — 90 days, 30 days, 7 days, or 1 day before expiry. Set multiple reminders for the same document.
Is Terminowo available on both iOS and Android?
Yes, Terminowo is available on both iOS and Android platforms.
Is my data stored securely?
Your documents and personal data are stored locally on your phone. No cloud storage, no accounts required, no data shared with third parties.
Is the app free to use?
Yes, Terminowo is completely free. No subscriptions, no hidden costs.
Can I edit a document after I've saved it?
Absolutely. You can update the name, category, expiry date, and reminder settings at any time.
