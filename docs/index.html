Privacy Policy

Terminowo — Last updated: February 19, 2026

Overview

Terminowo is a mobile application that helps you track expiry dates of insurance documents and other important papers by scanning them with your camera. This policy explains what data the app collects, how it is used, and your rights regarding that data.

Data Stored on Your Device

All user data is stored locally on your device and is never uploaded to any server operated by us.

  • Document images — photos you take with the in-app camera are saved to app-private storage on your device.
  • Document metadata — expiry dates, document names, categories, and reminder preferences are stored in a local database.

We do not operate any backend servers. If you uninstall the app, all locally stored data is permanently deleted.

Data Shared with Third-Party Services

Google Document AI

When you scan a document, the image is sent to Google Document AI for optical character recognition (OCR) to extract expiry dates. The image data is processed by Google in accordance with the Google Cloud Platform Terms of Service and Google Privacy Policy. Google Document AI does not use your data to train models.

Google Sign-In

The app uses Google Sign-In solely to obtain an authentication token for calling the Document AI service. We request access to your basic profile (name, email) and the cloud-platform scope. This information is used only for authentication and is not stored on any external server, shared with other parties, or used for any other purpose.

Data We Do NOT Collect

  • No analytics, crash reporting, or usage telemetry
  • No advertising identifiers or ad tracking
  • No location data
  • No contacts, call logs, or personal files outside the app
  • No data is transmitted to servers owned or operated by us

Permissions

  • Camera — required to scan documents.
  • Notifications — used to send expiry date reminders, scheduled locally on your device.
  • Internet — required to send document images to Google Document AI and to authenticate via Google Sign-In.

Data Retention and Deletion

Your data remains on your device for as long as the app is installed. You can delete individual documents within the app at any time. Uninstalling the app removes all data. Images sent to Google Document AI for OCR are processed transiently and are not retained by Google after processing is complete.

  • Data is not used for advertising or marketing.

  • Users can request deletion by uninstalling the app or deleting documents within the app.

Apple App Store

For the purposes of Apple's App Tracking Transparency framework: this app does not track you across other companies' apps or websites. No data is used for tracking as defined by Apple.

Children's Privacy

This app is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided data through the app, please contact us so we can address the concern.
