Account & Data Deletion

Terminowo — Last updated: February 19, 2026

How Your Data Is Stored

Terminowo stores all data locally on your device. We do not operate any backend servers and do not store any user accounts, documents, or personal information on our side.

How to Delete Your Data

Delete Individual Documents

Open any document in the app, tap the delete button, and confirm. This permanently removes the document image and all associated metadata (expiry date, reminders, category) from your device.

Sign Out of Google

Tap your profile icon on the home screen and select Sign Out. This removes the authentication token and your Google profile information (name, email) from the app's memory. No Google account data is retained after sign-out.

Delete All App Data

To remove all data at once, go to your device's Settings → Apps → Terminowo → Storage → Clear Data. This deletes all documents, images, preferences, and authentication state.

Uninstall the App

Uninstalling Terminowo permanently deletes all locally stored data, including document images, metadata, and any cached authentication tokens.

What Gets Deleted

Data type Deleted on sign-out Deleted on clear data / uninstall
Google profile (name, email) Yes Yes
Authentication token Yes Yes
Document images No Yes
Document metadata & reminders No Yes

Third-Party Data

Document images sent to Google Document AI for OCR are processed transiently and are not retained by Google after processing. To manage your Google account data and connected apps, visit Google Account Permissions and revoke access for Terminowo.
